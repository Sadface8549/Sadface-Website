My name is Josh, and this is my story.

My story starts with me sitting on my bed loading up my first anime, Sword Art Online. Seeing Kirito battle his way through the virtual world of Aincrad and finally escape; ignited my passion for working with computers. The day I enrolled in my first Python course at high school I knew that this was what I loved. They say it takes years to figure out your passion career, I suppose I got lucky.

With college right around the corner, I had to choose which path I took. The programming path or the networking path. I took the latter option and learned so much. I was comfortable with networking, and it came to me very easily, however, it was always lacking something. For my capstone project, I decided to take a leap and make a website, to code again. This website is the foundation of that and the ignition to keep pursuing programming. They say to follow your dreams, and that's what I'll do; However, this time that choice isn't an option anymore. I'll work to become a professional programmer and networker. Because, Life is not a game of luck. If you wanna win, work hard! -Sora
